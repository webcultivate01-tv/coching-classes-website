FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive

What is included in the 7-day free trial?▾
The 7-day free trial gives you full access to all lectures, study material, test series and doubt sessions of your chosen course. No payment required, cancel anytime before the trial ends.
Are the classes live or recorded?▾
Both! We offer live classes 6 days a week and all sessions are recorded and available for replay for 12 months. You'll never miss a class.
Do you have EMI or instalment payment options?▾
Yes! We offer 3, 6 and 12 month EMI options at 0% interest on all annual courses. Monthly subscription plans are also available for flexible learning.
How are doubt sessions conducted?▾
Doubts can be submitted via our app 24/7 and are answered within 4 hours. We also have live daily doubt sessions with subject teachers every evening from 7–9 PM.
Is there a scholarship test available?▾
Yes! We conduct a monthly Scholarship Test (EduRise SAT) where students can win upto 100% fee waiver. The test is free and open to all students above Class 9.
What is the refund policy?▾
We offer a full refund within 7 days of enrollment if you're not satisfied. No questions asked. After 7 days, partial refund is available based on usage.
