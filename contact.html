FAQ

Aksar Pooche Jaane Wale Sawaal

Free demo class mein kya hota hai?
+
Demo class mein aap ek full lecture attend kar sakte ho, faculty se mil sakte ho aur course ke baare mein poori jaankari le sakte ho. Koi commitment nahi, bilkul free.
Online aur offline dono options hain?
+
Haan! Hum live online classes, recorded lectures aur Nagpur mein offline classes — teeno options dete hain. Aap apni convenience ke hisaab se choose kar sakte ho.
EMI ya installment mein fees pay kar sakte hain?
+
Bilkul! Hum 3, 6 aur 12 month ki EMI options dete hain. Scholarship test mein qualify karne par 100% tak fee waiver bhi milta hai.
Study material kaise milega?
+
Enrollment ke baad aapko printed study material, digital notes aur practice sheets milenge. Saath mein online portal par 500+ recorded lectures bhi access kar sakte ho.
Doubt kaise clear karein?
+
24/7 doubt support available hai — WhatsApp, chat ya video call se. Weekly doubt sessions bhi hote hain jahan directly teachers se pooch sakte ho.
Course join karne ki last date kya hai?
+
New batches har month shuru hoti hain. Abhi enroll karo aur missed lectures ki recordings access karo. Seats limited hain — jaldi karo!
