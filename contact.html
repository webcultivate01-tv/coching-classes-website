FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens in a free demo class?
+
In the demo class, you can attend a full lecture, meet the faculty, and get complete information about the course. No commitment required — it's completely free.
Are both online and offline options available?
+
Yes! We offer live online classes, recorded lectures, and offline classes in Nagpur — all three options. You can choose whichever is most convenient for you.
Can I pay fees in EMI or installments?
+
Absolutely! We offer EMI options of 3, 6, and 12 months. Students who qualify in the scholarship test can also receive up to 100% fee waiver.
How will I receive study material?
+
After enrollment, you will receive printed study material, digital notes, and practice sheets. You can also access 500+ recorded lectures on our online portal.
How can I clear my doubts?
+
24/7 doubt support is available via WhatsApp, chat, or video call. Weekly doubt-clearing sessions are also held where you can ask teachers directly.
What is the last date to join a course?
+
New batches start every month. Enroll now and access recordings of missed lectures. Seats are limited — act fast!
