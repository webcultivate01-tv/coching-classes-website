Our Story

From a Dream to a Mission

EduRise Academy was founded in 2015 in a small classroom in Nagpur. Our founder Rajesh Sharma — himself an IIT topper — noticed that talented students were falling behind simply due to lack of proper guidance.

His vision was to build a platform where every student — regardless of their background — could access world-class coaching. Today, 10 years later, we are part of the aspirations of 15,000+ students.

Our 50+ expert faculty members, alumni of IIT, AIIMS, and top universities, give their best every day to ensure your selection. This is not just a coaching institute — it's a family.
