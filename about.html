EduRise Academy was founded with a single mission — to make world-class coaching accessible to every student, regardless of background or location.

Our Story

From a Small Classroom to India's Trusted Platform

It started in 2018 with just 40 students and a dream — that every student in Nagpur deserves access to the same quality of coaching that students in big metros enjoy.

Our founder, a JEE topper from IIT Bombay, saw firsthand how students in Tier-2 & Tier-3 cities struggled to find quality guidance. EduRise Academy was born to bridge that gap.

Today, we've helped 15,000+ students from across Maharashtra and beyond achieve their dream ranks in JEE, NEET, UPSC, and board exams — and we're just getting started.
