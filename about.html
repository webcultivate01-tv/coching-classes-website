Our Story

Ek Sapne Se Ek Mission Tak

EduRise Academy ki shuruat 2015 mein Nagpur mein ek choti si classroom se hui thi. Hamare founder Rajesh Sharma — khud ek IIT topper — ne dekha ki talented students sirf sahi guidance ki kami ki wajah se peeche reh jaate hain.

Unka vision tha: ek aisa platform banao jahan har student — chahe woh kisi bhi background se ho — world-class coaching pa sake. Aaj, 10 saalon baad, hum 15,000+ students ke sapnon ka hissa hain.

Hamare 50+ expert faculty members, jo IIT, AIIMS aur top universities ke alumni hain, roz apna best dete hain taaki aapka selection pakka ho. Yeh sirf ek coaching nahi — yeh ek family hai.
